I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science (2010) and M.S. degree in Information Security (2012) from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NUPT), China, and received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering (2017) from Northeastern University, Boston, MA.

From 2017-2018, I worked as a Data Scientist at the Big Data Experience Lab, Adobe Research, San Jose, CA.

From 2012-2017, I worked as a Research Assistant in the SMILE Lab at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun (Raymond) Fu. 

In Summer 2014 and 2015, I was a Data Scientist Intern at Adobe Research, working with Dr. Jaya Kawale and Dr. Nikos Vlassis on user behavior prediction and causal inference models for digital marketing. 

From 2007 to 2012, I was a Research Assistant in the Pattern Recognition Lab at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, working with Prof. Xiaoyuan Jing on pattern recognition and biometrics.
